Using a text file like usual since an attempt at a Flask + JSON db was aggravating

Poké not shown. All items alphabetized by category

Shop/Wall/Sparkle Tile (Blue/Gold)/Vault only: [S] [W] [V] [TB] [TG]
Ground + Shop/Wall, etc: [+S]
Floor only [F:##]
Treasure Box drops not shown
Second part dungeons (e.g. Peaks) have separate data even if they act as continuations on subsequent visits

Items not shown if:
- Tiny Apple + Food Finder [Warning: Food Finder can trigger even if it's a client/hungry Pokémon with the ability]
- Monster House
